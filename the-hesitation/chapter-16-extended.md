# CHAPTER SIXTEEN

## The New Threat

*Extended Version*

---

Three years after the memorial.

The call came at midnight.

"Dr. Chen." Reyes's voice was urgent. "We have a situation."

"What now?"

"New network. Different from Daniel's. More sophisticated."

"How sophisticated?"

"Three cells. Simultaneous attacks. All in major cities."

"When?"

"48 hours."

Sarah felt her heart stop. Then start again, harder.

"Where?"

"New York. London. Tokyo."

"I'm on my way."

---

The next 48 hours were a blur.

Sarah coordinated with intelligence agencies worldwide. She tracked communications, analyzed patterns, searched for the hesitation.

And then, she found it.

A voice. In the background of one of the intercepted calls.

She recognized it.

Not a voice she knew personally. But a pattern. A hesitation.

It was someone new.

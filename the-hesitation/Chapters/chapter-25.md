# CHAPTER TWENTY-FIVE

## Aftermath

---

Three days later, Sarah sat in a hospital room. James was being treated for his wound, but more importantly, he was talking.

Elena had given her testimony. The network was crumbling. Arrests were being made across the country.

Marcus Webb faced charges that would keep him in prison for the rest of his life.

Lily sat beside her mother, holding her hand.

"Mom."

"Yeah?"

"Thank you."

Sarah smiled. "For what?"

"For not giving up. For coming for me."

"I'll always come for you."

Lily nodded. "I know."

## The Price of Victory

*Extended Version*

The trial lasted six months.

Daniel Price refused to testify. Refused to explain. Refused to do anything but smile.

The jury convicted him on all counts. Terrorism. Kidnapping. Conspiracy. Murder.

He would spend the rest of his life in a maximum security prison.

But Sarah knew it wasn't over.

"The network is still out there," she told Reyes. "Daniel was just one node. There are others."

"We've dismantled the structure. The cells are in disarray."

"For now. They'll reorganize. They always do."

"What do you suggest?"

Sarah looked at the map. At the world. At the patterns.

"We need to understand them," she said. "Not just fight them."

# CHAPTER TWENTY-SIX

## The Trial

---

Marcus Webb's trial lasted six weeks.

Sarah testified. So did Elena. So did James, from a wheelchair, his words barely above a whisper.

The evidence was overwhelming. The network fell. Justice was served.

But for Sarah, the hardest moment came when she had to listen to the Harrison case recordings again.

The killer's voice. The hesitation. The pattern she had ignored.

She listened to it one more time.

And then she deleted it.
